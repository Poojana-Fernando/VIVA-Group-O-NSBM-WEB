Heart Centre

Our Heart Centre supports students and staff with heart-related consultations, basic cardiac screening, and guidance for a healthier lifestyle.

  • Blood pressure and pulse checks
  • Chest pain / palpitations initial assessment
  • Health advice: diet, exercise, stress management
  • Referrals to specialist care when needed

The heart is a muscular organ that pumps blood throughout the body, supplying oxygen and nutrients while removing waste products. It plays a crucial role in the circulatory system and is essential for maintaining overall health and well-being. The heart consists of four chambers: the left and right atria, and the left and right ventricles. It beats approximately 100,000 times per day, circulating about 5 liters of blood every minute. Proper heart function is vital for sustaining life, and maintaining a healthy lifestyle can help prevent cardiovascular diseases and promote long-term heart health.
