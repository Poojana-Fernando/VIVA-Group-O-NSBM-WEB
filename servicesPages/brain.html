Brain & Spine

Brain and spine care often referred to as neuroscience or neurological care—focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of disorders affecting the central nervous system (CNS). Because the CNS acts as the body's command center, this field is highly specialized and requires a multidisciplinary approach.

  • The Brain: The control center for memory, emotion, motor skills, and vital functions like breathing. Care focuses on everything from tumors and strokes to degenerative diseases.
  • The Spine: The highway for nerve signals between the brain and the rest of the body. Care involves the vertebrae (bones), discs (cushions), and the spinal cord itself.
